Destination

Bhedetar

Bhedetar, located in Dhankuta district, is a destination perfect for the summer heat.It is cool climate, stunning sunrise and sunset views, Bhedetar offers a refreshing getaway.

Kanyam

kanyam or Ilam, located in eastern Nepal, is a popular tourist destination known for its scenic tea gardens, lush green landscapes, and stunning mountain views.

Dharan

Dharan is a sub-metropolitan city in Sunsari District, Koshi Province, Nepal. It was established as a municipality in 1958 and has grown into one of the largest cities in eastern Nepal.

Damak

Damak is a municipality located in Koshi Province, Nepal. It's one of the oldest municipalities in the Jhapa District and is known for its motto, "Green City, Prosperous Damak.

Koshi Tappu

Koshi Tappu Wildlife Reserve, established in 1976 and it's a stunning 175-square-kilometer wetland sanctuary located along the Koshi River in eastern Nepal..

Taplejung

Taplejung's geography is diverse, ranging from subtropical valleys to alpine forests, and it is rich in rivers, waterfalls, and wildlife. The Kanchenjunga Conservation Area within the Moutain hills.
