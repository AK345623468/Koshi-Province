Transportation

Bus

Transportation refers to the movement of passengers using buses, which are large, self-propelled vehicles designed to carry multiple people, typically along fixed routes.

Air plane

Koshi Province in Nepal has several airports that facilitate air travel. One airport is, located in Municipality of Solukhumbu District.

Car

A car is a four-wheeled motor vehicle used for personal transportation. When we talk about car travel, it simply means traveling by car as a mode of transportation.
